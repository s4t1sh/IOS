Modular Manufacturing

All kind of modular furniture including 25mm, 50mm & 75 mm desk base system / full height partitions made out of powder coated aluminum extrusions, pre₃laminated board tiles, fabric wrapped MDF tiles with single / double channels for power & data cables, Post₃formed table tops & mobile pedestal units etc can be fabricated as per the required design.

We offer wide range furniture from contemporary to modern & a spectrum of office furniture viz., workstations, partitions, computer tables, storage and filing cabinets, executive tables, board room table etc., and furniture, which could be made to order, to suit exactly the requirements and tastes of our clients. We also specialize in all kinds of commercial and residential furniture.
